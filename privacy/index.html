Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

1. About this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FILMERA ("FILMERA," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the FILMERA mobile application and related services.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and encrypted password credentials.
  • Movie preferences: movie likes, dislikes, filters, room participation, and movie matches.
  • Usage and technical information: app interactions, request information, device or browser type, IP address, diagnostic information, and server logs that may be processed by our hosting providers.
  • Support communications: information you provide when contacting us about privacy, support, or account issues.

3. Why we use this information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage your FILMERA account.
  • Authenticate you and protect account access.
  • Create rooms, connect participants, record swipes, and generate movie matches.
  • Provide password reset and account support.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse.

4. Storage and security

Account information, room participation, and movie preference data are stored using MongoDB Atlas. Passwords are stored only as one-way cryptographic hashes, not as readable passwords.

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including encrypted network connections and authenticated access. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Service providers and sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or process limited information with service providers that help operate FILMERA:

  • MongoDB Atlas: database hosting and storage.
  • Render: backend hosting, networking, and server logs.
  • Vercel: website hosting, networking, and related technical logs.
  • TMDB: movie metadata, images, and availability information. FILMERA sends movie-related requests to TMDB but does not intentionally send your name, email address, or password.

These providers process information under their own terms and privacy policies. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect users, FILMERA, or the public.

6. Data retention

We retain account and preference information while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Some limited records may remain temporarily in backups, security logs, or where retention is required by law.

7. Account and data deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app by opening Account Settings and selecting Delete Account. This permanently deletes your user profile and swipe history and removes your account from rooms.

You may also request access, correction, or deletion by emailing privacy@filmera.us from the email address associated with your account. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

8. Children's privacy

FILMERA is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it.

9. International processing

Our service providers may process information in the United States or other countries. Privacy and data protection laws may differ from those in your location.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as FILMERA changes. We will publish the revised policy on this page and update the effective date. Material changes may also be communicated in the app or through another appropriate channel.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: privacy@filmera.us.
